7. SIKKIM

Sikkim is the paradise nestled in the North-East part of India with the magnetic views of the great Himalayas. If you havenâ€™t thought of exploring the beauty of this magnificent state then you are truly missing on something really beautiful. Sikkim is like wonders in a small package with bewitching meadows, snowcapped and sky-piercing Himalayas, and the mesmeric charm of its vibrant culture.

BEST TIME TO VISIT: October to January

HOW TO REACH:

  • By air: There is no airport in Sikkim itself so you will have to travel to the nearest airport in Bagdogra in West Bengal. This airport is about 124 kilometers from Gangtok and it takes about 5 hours from Bagdogra by buses or cabs.
  • By rail:You will have to travel to New Jalpaiguri and Siliguri rail stations. Sikkim does not have its own station and these are the two nearest rail stations located in West Bengal.
  • By road: You can travel by taxi or cab from the nearest states that are Siliguri and Darjeeling, both in West Bengal. This is a scenic route as well as the Teesta river flows on one side while the other side is encompassed by lush green mountains.
ATTRACTIONS: Nathula Pass, Yumthang, Tsomgo Lake, Zuluk Loops, Nathang Valley, Zero Point Sikkim.
  • THINGS TO DO:Helicopter Ride: Admire The Views From The Sky, Mountain Biking: Get An Adrenaline Rush, Camping: All About Bonfire & Sceneries, Nature Tours: Explore The Heart Of Sikkim, Rock Climbing: Test Your Strength, Stargazing: For Uncountable Twinkling Stars, Ropeway: Witness The City From Up Top, Cycling: Let The Crisp Air Brush Past You, Bungee Jumping: Thrill At Its Best.
  • PLACES TO VISIT: Gangtok, Pelling, Lachung, Lachen and Yumthang Valley, Yuksom, Nathula Pass, Banjhakri Waterfall and Energy Park.
  • FAMOUS MARKETS:MG Road, Lal Bazaar, New Market, Old Market, Directorate of Handicrafts & Handlooms.
  • BEST RESORTS: Udaan Alpine Resort, WelcomHeritage Resort Denzong Regency, Orange Village Resort, Norbu Ghang Resort, Pachhu Village Resort, Cherry Resort.
