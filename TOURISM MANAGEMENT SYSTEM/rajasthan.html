2. RAJASTHAN

Rajasthan, known formerly by the name Rajputana or the land of the kings, is the largest state in the republic of India. It covers an area of 342,239 sq kilometers which is 10.4% of India. However despite of its large size it is covered by the Great Indian Desert, Thar. The Thar Desert runs parallel to the Sutlej-Indus river valley. Being located at the forefront of Indian civilization, the state of Rajasthan has seen the age of kings thus, explaining the moniker ‘the land of kings’. Rajasthan houses an amazing display of arts and architecture, courtesy of it being a princely state.

BEST TIME TO VISIT: October to March

HOW TO REACH:

  • By air: The main airport in Rajasthan is the Jaipur International Airport. Besides this, there are two other airports in Udaipur and Jodhpur. There are also a number of domestic airports in Rajasthan from where you can catch flights easily from one city to another. The list of domestic airports in Rajasthan includes Udaipur Airport (Maharana Pratap Airport), Jodhpur Airport (Civil Airport), Kota Airport, Jaisalmer Airport, Nal Airport (Bikaner Airport), Phalodi Air Force Station, and Suratgarh Air Force Station.
  • By rail:Rajasthan is also well-connected by rail and the major stations here are in Jodhpur, Jaipur, Udaipur, and Ajmer. Though, the busiest railway station of Rajasthan is in Jaipur.Ajmer Jn (AII), Udaipur City (UDZ), Bikaner, and Jodhpur Jn (JU) are the popular railway stations in Rajasthan.
  • By road:Rajasthan has twenty national highways, which ensure a smooth travelling experience. The most well-connected national highway in Rajasthan is NH8 that connects to Delhi, Udaipur, Jaipur, Vadodara, Ahmedabad, Mumbai, and many other cities.
ATTRACTIONS: Mount Abu/Dilwara Jain Temples, The Gajner Palace at Bikaner, Desert safari, Jain temples at Lodhruva, Abandoned village of Kuldhara, Go birding at Desert National park, Bharatpur–Keoladeo National Park.
  • THINGS TO DO:Enjoy cultural events in Neemrana Fort, Do a zip lining tour of the town, Spot wildlife in Sariska National Park, Take a tour of the city museum, Attend the famous Camel Festival, Shop your heart out at Kote Gate, Take a tour of the majestic Junagarh Fort, Visit the popular Karni Mata Temple,, Witness the splendour of the Lalgarh Palace, Enjoy a camel safari in the Thar.
  • PLACES TO VISIT: Jaisalmer, Jaipur, Jodhpur, Bundi, Bikaner, Ajmer, Pushkar, Udaipur, Mount Abu
  • FAMOUS MARKETS: Hathi Pol Bazar, Tripolia Bazar, Johari Bazaar, Bapu Bazar, Sadar Bazaar, Nai Sarak, Kote Gate, Pansari Bazaar, Bada Bazaar, Sarafa Bazaar, Mahatma Gandhi Road.
  • BEST RESORTS:The Country Side Resort, Bamboo Saa Resort & Spa, The Ananta Udaipur, Radisson Blu Udaipur Palace Resort & Spa, Mahua Bagh Resort, The Tigress, Ranthambhore.
