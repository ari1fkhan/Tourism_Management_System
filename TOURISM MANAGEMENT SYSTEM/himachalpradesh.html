8. HIMACHAL PRADESH

Himachal Pradesh is synonymous with scenic beauty, serene landscape, and adventure sports. Literally, Himachal means “the land of snow”. Situated in the western Himalaya, Himachal Pradesh has several hill stations which are the most appropriate destination to find respite from the scorching summer heat.

BEST TIME TO VISIT: February to June

HOW TO REACH:

  • By air: There are three domestic airports that connect Himachal Pradesh to the rest of the country. These are located at Kullu-Manali, Dharamshala and Shimla. The airports have flights from Himachal Pradesh to Delhi and Chandigarh.
  • By rail:The important railway stations in Himachal are Solan and Una. There is also a toy train available that connects Himachal to Kalka. Opting for a train proves to be great option as they are usually affordable, comfortable, well maintained and a lot more fun than a flight journey.
  • By road:The roads are very well maintained and even during the monsoon season, it is safe to drive up to the state, just needs a little caution. Himachal Pradesh is very well connected to the major cities.
ATTRACTIONS: Solang Valley, Jakhu Temple, Beas River, Manikaran Gurudwara, Jogini Waterfall, Spiti Valley, The Mall.
  • THINGS TO DO: Triund Hill: Go Trek This Heavenly Place, Great Himalayan National Park: For A Nature Meet, Khajjiar Lake: Explore The Mini Switzerland Of India, Kullu Dussehra: Seek Blessings, Naldehra Golf Course: Test Your Golf Skills, Dharmasala: Appreciate The Tibetan Opera, Hanuman Tibba: Go Heli-Skiing, Bir Billing: Fly High In The Skies, Green Shopping: Explore The Shops In McLeod Ganj.
  • PLACES TO VISIT:Kullu-Manali, Shimla, Dharamshala, Kasauli,Bir Billing, Malana,Dalhousie, Spiti + Tabo + Kaza, Kasol, Khajjiar, Mashobra, Palampur, Kinnaur.
  • FAMOUS MARKETS:Himachal Emporium, Tibetan Market, Lower Bazaar, Lakkar Bazaar, Akhara Bazaar, Sultanpur Market, Old Manali Market, Kotwali Bazaar, Jogibara Road Bazaar.
  • BEST RESORTS:Maple Resort By Aamod, Timber Trail Resort, Araiya Palampur, WelcomHeritage Urvashi's Retreat, Renest River Country Resort, Flag House Resort, Banjara Mountain Retreat Chail Hills.
