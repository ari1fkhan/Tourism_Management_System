9. ANDAMAN AND NICOBAR

Andaman and Nicobar Islands, also popular as Kalapani is one of the most beautiful places to visit in India. Consisting of 572 Islands, this place is the perfect getaway for tourists who love to explore the marine world. Out of these 572 Islands, 50 are inhabited by the people where the rest of the Islands are either not that large for inhabitants, come under the forest reserve or it is used by the military of India as their base.

BEST TIME TO VISIT: October to March

HOW TO REACH:

  • By air:The closest airport is located in Port Blair. You can find direct flights from many Indian inland regions like Delhi, Chennai, Bangalore, Mumbai, and others.
  • By ship:Kolkata, Chennai, and Visakhapatnam are the top portal regions from where you can find ships to Andaman. The ship services are available only three or four times per month and it would take around 60-65 hours to reach Andaman via water travel.
  • By rail:Andaman is not connected via railway with any part of the world.
  • By road:There are no roadways connecting Andaman with any part of the world.
ATTRACTIONS: Cellular Jail National Memorial, Radhanagar Beach, Havelock Island, Mount Harriet And Madhuban, Museum Circuit, Rajiv Gandhi Water Sports Complex, North Bay Beach, Seafood, Mahatma Gandhi Marine National Park, Diglipur, Chidiya Tapu, Ross Island, Neil Island, Baratang Island, Elephant Beach, Barren Island.
  • THINGS TO DO:Beach Bumming, Water Activities, Cellular Jail, Coral Reefs, Birdwatching.
  • PLACES TO VISIT:Havelock Island, Port Blair, Neil Island, Baratang Island, Radhanagar Beach, Diglipur, Chidiya Tapu, Barren Island, Cellular Jail, Ross Island, Elephant Beach, Bharatpur Beach, Jolly Buoy Island, Kala Patthar.
  • FAMOUS MARKETS:Sagarika Government Emporium, Aberdeen Bazaar, Queen Sea Covering Craft, Electrical Plaza, Sagarika.
  • BEST RESORTS:Holiday Home, Havelock Island Beach Resort, Sea Shell Samssara, Welcomhotel Bay Island Port Blair, Sandhya Residency, Peerless Resort Portblair.
